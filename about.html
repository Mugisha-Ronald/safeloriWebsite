Who We Are

SafeLori is a technology and financial logistical company that connects logistical companies and truck drivers to customers. Our technology increases efficiency, cost reduction, decarbonization in the supply chain, thereby reducing time, money and emissions through both the normal trucks and fully clean and electric cargo truck transport in Africa.


SafeLori is developing an ecosystem for the informal entrepreneurs (mass market) to grow and thrive with us as their partner and this will enable the informal drivers to; Own income-generating assets (Fully Electric Cargo Trucks),Build a purely clean and green logistical network throughout Africa,Earn future growth opportunities through the SafeLori digital platform
