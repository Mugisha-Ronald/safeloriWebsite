Who We Are

SafeLori is a technology and financial logistical company that connects logistical companies and truck drivers to customers. Our technology increases efficiency, cost reduction, decarbonization in the supply chain, thereby reducing time, money and emissions through both the normal trucks and fully clean and electric cargo truck transport in Africa.
